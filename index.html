Our Partner

Stop APPI Hate is a non-porfit organization that fights and documents racism and racial injustice targeting Asian-Americans and Pacific Islanders. Founded in 2020, Stop AAPI Hate is a respnse to the icreasing hate crimes agaisnt Asian-Americans during the Covid-19 pandemic. Stop AAPI Hate’s core strategies are 1) Data & Research, 2) Policy & Advocacy, 3) Community Capacity Building, and 4) Narrative Change.
